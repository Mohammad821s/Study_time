WORK TIME
About

Work Time is a productivity app that helps you study and stay focused. It combines three tools in one place: a focus alarm, a task list, and a pet companion that grows with your progress.

The Focus Alarm keeps you alert by ringing at set intervals. You must silence it manually every time, which keeps you present and engaged. You earn 1 coin for every minute you spend in a focused session.

The To-Do List lets you track your tasks. Complete a task and earn 5 coins. Tasks reset automatically every 24 hours so your list stays fresh every day.

Coins you earn can be spent in the Shop on hats and shirts for your rabbit companion, who lives in your Home page and wears whatever you dress him in.
