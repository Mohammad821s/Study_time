About

Focus Alarm is a productivity tool designed to keep you mentally engaged. Unlike a regular alarm, it forces you to actively respond — you must silence it manually every time it rings.

This creates a cycle of alertness and anticipation, training your mind to stay present rather than drift into passive waiting.

Set your interval, choose your tone, and let the rhythm of the alarm structure your focus session. Two controls keep you in charge: silence the current ring, or end the repeat cycle entirely.
